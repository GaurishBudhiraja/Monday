# Future Plugins Specification

Version: 1.0

Status: Visionary

Owner: MONDAY Research Team

---

# Purpose

Future Plugins define the long-term expansion roadmap of MONDAY beyond traditional desktop computing.

The objective is to ensure that MONDAY's architecture remains adaptable to future technologies, intelligent environments, robotics, distributed AI systems, edge computing, augmented reality, quantum computing, and emerging interaction models without requiring architectural redesign.

Future Plugins are not tied to current hardware or software vendors.

They define capabilities that MONDAY may support as technology evolves.

---

# Design Philosophy

Architecture should outlive technology.

Platforms change.

Capabilities remain.

MONDAY must evolve continuously without changing its core architecture.

Future Plugins remain capability-based.

Planning remains inside the Mission System.

Providers remain replaceable.

Capabilities remain stable.

Security remains mandatory.

Every future technology integrates through the Plugin System.

Future Plugins ensure MONDAY remains adaptable to technologies that do not yet exist while preserving the architectural integrity of MONDAY Core.
