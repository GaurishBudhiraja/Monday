# MONDAY AI Operating System (AIOS)

# Enterprise Plugins Specification

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Owner: Enterprise Platform Team

---

# Purpose

Enterprise Plugins enable MONDAY to integrate with enterprise software ecosystems, workplace collaboration tools, IT service management platforms, customer relationship management systems, observability platforms, identity providers, and business workflows.

The objective is to make MONDAY capable of operating inside professional environments while remaining provider-independent and capability-driven.

Enterprise platforms become intelligent operating environments coordinated by the Mission System.

---

# Design Philosophy

Business workflows are missions.

Enterprise software provides capabilities.

Enterprise Plugins expose standardized capabilities regardless of vendor implementation.

Planning remains inside MONDAY Core.

Execution belongs to Enterprise Plugins.
