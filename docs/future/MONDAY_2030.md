Version: 1.0

# Introduction

MONDAY is not intended to become another chatbot.

It is not a better prompt interface.

It is not simply an AI coding assistant.

MONDAY is designed to become an Artificial Intelligence Operating System.

An operating system coordinates hardware, software, users, resources, applications, security, and execution.

MONDAY extends that concept into the age of Artificial Intelligence.

Instead of managing only processes, it manages intelligence.

Instead of launching applications, it executes Missions.

Instead of isolated conversations, it develops long-term knowledge.

Instead of reacting, it plans.

Instead of remembering chats, it builds understanding.

This document defines that vision.

---

# The Mission

Build an AI Operating System that becomes the primary interface between humans, software, knowledge, and intelligent automation.

MONDAY should help people think, build, learn, automate, create, and solve problems without replacing human judgment.

The objective is augmentation, not replacement.

# Closing Statement

MONDAY is an attempt to rethink what an operating system can become in the era of Artificial Intelligence.

Rather than organizing files and processes alone, it organizes knowledge, reasoning, memory, planning, and intelligent execution.

Its purpose is not to answer every question.

Its purpose is to help people accomplish meaningful work with confidence, transparency, and control.

This vision will continue to evolve, but its foundation remains constant:

Build an AI Operating System that augments human intelligence while remaining open, modular, secure, explainable, and worthy of long-term trust.
